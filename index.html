About Me

I am a geophysicist working in the fields of GNSS, InSAR, Seismology, Crustal Deformation and Reservoir Triggered Seismicity.

My research focuses on understanding active tectonics, strain accumulation and deformation processes using modern geodetic and seismological techniques.
